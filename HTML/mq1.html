Ranip

Ranip is a well-established and evolving residential area of Ahmedabad, offering a healthy mix of urban convenience and suburban charm. Its connectivity, infrastructure, and community-centric vibe make it especially attractive to families and investors.

Facts of Ranip:

  • Ranip is a residential area in North Ahmedabad, well-connected by the Ring Road and Metro.

  • It is divided into Old Ranip and New Ranip, with New Ranip being a modern housing hub.

  • The area is popular for its affordable housing, schools, hospitals, and shopping centers.
